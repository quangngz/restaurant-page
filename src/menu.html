Signature Dishes

Handmade Tuscan Pappardelle

Executive Chef • Renata Moretti

Delicate wide ribbons of pappardelle pasta, hand-rolled daily and served with slow-braised wild boar ragu. Inspired by traditional Tuscan methods and refined under Massimo Bottura’s mentorship.

Gochujang Glazed Short Ribs

Head Chef • Marcus Lin

Rich Korean-style beef short ribs glazed with house-fermented gochujang and roasted low-and-slow. Paired with grilled seasonal vegetables from California farms for a modern East-West fusion.

Spinach Ricotta Gnudi

Executive Chef • Amara Singh

Pillowy gnudi made from fresh ricotta and spinach, lightly coated in semolina, and simmered in a sage-butter sauce. Reflects Tuscan traditions with precise French technique from Le Cordon Bleu.

Golden Lentil Kitchari Bowl

Private Chef • Amara Singh

A warming Ayurvedic dish made from yellow lentils and basmati rice, infused with turmeric, ginger, and ghee. Balanced for all doshas and garnished with coconut chutney and toasted cumin seeds.

Liquid Core Mille-Feuille

Pastry Chef • Théo Marchand

A modern twist on the French classic, with razor-thin caramelized pastry layers and a rich vanilla custard center that oozes upon slicing. Created using techniques learned under Pierre Hermé at La Flèche d'Or.
